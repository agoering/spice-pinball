Welcome to SPICE Engineering!

{% include docs_contents_mobile.html %}

This is the website for the SPICE Engineering camp, a summer science camp for middle-school kids, being conducted by the Oregon Center for Optics and partners on the campus of the University of Oregon.

During the camp, participants design and build pinball tables using basic electronic sensors and circuits. Yes, we know, that's awesome!

Join in on the fun by checking out our tutorials. For more information on how to install the SPICE Pinball Arduino Library and Sketchbook, suggestions on sourcing electronic components, and ideas on ways to use and remix the camp content, check out the Teach tab!

Finally, if you live in or near Eugene, OR and want to attend camp, visit the SPICE mothership for application details.
