This is the website for the SPICE Engineering camp, a summer science camp conducted by the Oregon Center for Optics on the campus of the University of Oregon.

During the camp, participants design and build pinball tables using basic electronic sensors and circuits!

Learn more about SPICE.

Join in on the fun by checking out our tutorials in the Learn tab! For more information on where to download the Arduino libraries and code used in the tutorials, suggestions on sourcing electronic components, and ideas on other ways to use the material, check out the Teach tab!
